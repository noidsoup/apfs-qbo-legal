Privacy Policy

This policy explains how APFS Database App handles data when syncing QuickBooks Online expense records to Airtable.

Data We Access

The app may access accounting transaction metadata from QuickBooks Online, including vendor names, transaction dates, memos, currencies, and amounts.

How Data Is Used

Data is used only for internal reporting and operational workflows in Airtable.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal or financial data. Data is only processed through authorized providers required for app functionality.

Security

Access tokens and API credentials are stored in secure secret managers and are restricted to authorized users.

Retention

Data is retained as needed for business and accounting records, or as required by law.

Your Choices

You can revoke QuickBooks access by disconnecting the app from QuickBooks Online.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact: support@aplaceforseniors.org
